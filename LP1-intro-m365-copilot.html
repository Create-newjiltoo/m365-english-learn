Overview

Microsoft 365 Copilot is an AI-powered productivity tool that integrates with Microsoft Graph and Microsoft 365 Apps to provide real-time intelligent assistance. This module examines the key tasks that administrators must complete as they prepare for Microsoft 365 Copilot.

These tasks include implementing SharePoint Advanced Management tools, configuring the Microsoft 365 security and compliance controls to protect your data, assigning Copilot licenses, and using Copilot agents to extend Microsoft 365 Copilot. You also explore how to drive Copilot adoption throughout your organization.

Microsoft 365 Copilot은 Microsoft Graph 및 Microsoft 365 앱과 통합(integrates)되어 실시간 지능형 지원을 제공하는 AI 기반 생산성(productivity) 도구입니다.

주요 작업: SharePoint 고급 관리 도구 구현, 보안 및 컴플라이언스(compliance) 제어 구성, Copilot 라이선스(licenses) 할당, 조직 전체 Copilot 채택(adoption) 추진.

📌 Scenario – Contoso

Contoso is excited to roll out Microsoft 365 Copilot across the organization. As a first step, it plans to ensure that it meets all prerequisites. Contoso sees Microsoft 365 Copilot as an optimal solution that can augment human capabilities with AI, leading to greater efficiency, collaboration, and insights.

Contoso는 조직 전체에 Copilot을 도입(roll out)합니다. 모든 필수 조건(prerequisites)을 충족하고, AI로 인간 역량을 강화(augment)하여 효율성·협업(collaboration)·인사이트(insights)를 높일 계획입니다.

What is Microsoft 365 Copilot?

⏱8분🎯Beginner📌Microsoft Learn
💡 파란 밑줄 단어를 터치하면 뜻이 팝업됩니다!

A New Paradigm in Work

Microsoft 365 Copilot represents a new paradigm in work, where employees collaborate with AI for increased productivity. Powered by Large Language Models (LLMs) and utilizing your business data from Microsoft Graph, Copilot integrates seamlessly with Teams, Word, Outlook, PowerPoint, Excel, and more.

Examples in action:

  • Outlook: Summarize the content of a large email thread.
  • PowerPoint: Create a presentation based on a report.
  • Word: Rewrite a paragraph in a different tone or style.
  • Teams: Summarize meetings and chat threads.

Copilot은 직원들이 AI와 협업(collaborate)하여 생산성(productivity)을 높이는 새로운 패러다임(paradigm)입니다. Microsoft Graph 비즈니스 데이터를 활용해 Microsoft 365 앱 전반에 원활하게(seamlessly) 통합됩니다.

  • Outlook: 긴 이메일 스레드 요약
  • PowerPoint: 보고서 기반 프레젠테이션 생성
  • Word: 문단을 다른 어조로 재작성
  • Teams: 회의 및 채팅 스레드 요약

The M365 Copilot System

Microsoft 365 Copilot is more than ChatGPT embedded in Microsoft 365. It is a sophisticated orchestration engine. It melds the strengths of LLMs with Microsoft 365 apps and your proprietary business data within Microsoft Graph.

Copilot은 단순히 ChatGPT를 내장한 것이 아닌, 정교한(sophisticated) 오케스트레이션(orchestration) 엔진입니다. LLM의 강점, Microsoft 365 앱, 그리고 독점적인(proprietary) 비즈니스 데이터를 융합합니다.

🔒 Trusted Data Security

🔒 Security

Copilot maintains a firm commitment to security, compliance, and privacy. It delivers precise, pertinent, and contextually aware responses. Since Copilot is integrated into Microsoft 365, it automatically aligns with your company's security, compliance, and privacy policies.

Copilot은 보안, 규정 준수(compliance), 개인정보 보호에 확고한 의지를 가집니다. 적절한(pertinent), 문맥에 맞는(contextually) 응답을 제공하며 Microsoft 365 정책과 자동으로 맞춰집니다.

✅ Prerequisites

Before you can access Microsoft 365 Copilot, you must meet these requirements:

🔑 Microsoft Entra ID

Users must have Entra ID accounts. Add or sync users via the onboarding wizard.

📄 Copilot Licensing

Qualifying Microsoft 365 plan needed. Copilot Chat is free; full Copilot requires add-on license.

💻 Microsoft 365 Apps

Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Teams required. Web versions work when license is assigned.

☁️ OneDrive Account

Required for file restore and OneDrive management features.

📧 New Outlook

New Outlook for Windows/Mac/Web/Mobile recommended for seamless integration.

💬 Microsoft Teams

Available on all platforms. Transcription must be enabled for meeting references.

🔄 Microsoft Loop

Must be enabled for your tenant via Microsoft 365 admin center.

🖊️ Whiteboard

Must be enabled for your tenant to use Copilot with Whiteboard.

  • 🔑 Microsoft Entra ID: 사용자 계정 필수
  • 📄 라이선스: Copilot Chat 무료 / 전체 Copilot 추가 라이선스 필요
  • 💻 Microsoft 365 앱: Word/Excel/PowerPoint/Outlook/Teams 필요
  • ☁️ OneDrive: 일부 기능에 필수
  • 📧 새 Outlook 권장 / 💬 Teams: 전사(transcription) 활성화 필요
  • 🔄 Loop / 🖊️ Whiteboard: 테넌트에서 활성화 필요

Explore How Microsoft 365 Copilot Works

⏱7분🎯Beginner📌Microsoft Learn
💡 파란 밑줄 단어를 터치하면 뜻이 팝업됩니다!

Understanding Context

At its core, Microsoft 365 Copilot is an intelligent partner that accompanies you throughout your day-to-day Microsoft 365 interactions. Copilot's goal is to save you time by generating new content, offering relevant suggestions, and making you more productive.

Copilot's effectiveness stems from understanding you:

  • Analyzing content. Copilot scrutinizes subject matter, tone, structure, and semantics to determine your intent.
  • Getting context from your work data. Your communications and activity history help Copilot get real-time context as it responds to your prompts.

Copilot은 일상적인 Microsoft 365 사용 전반에 동반하는 지능형 파트너입니다. 이해 방법:

  • 콘텐츠 분석: 주제·어조·구조·의미론(semantics)을 면밀히 검토
  • 업무 데이터 컨텍스트: 커뮤니케이션·활동 이력을 실시간으로 활용해 프롬프트(prompts)에 응답

What Copilot Can Do

  • Enhanced meeting engagement. Stay more engaged and get quick catch-ups for missed meetings.
  • Efficient email management. Summarize lengthy email threads and draft responses.
  • Writing assistance. Draft, edit, summarize, and create content alongside you.
  • Presentation development. Start a new presentation using natural language commands.
  • Data analysis and visualization. Identify trends, create visualizations, provide recommendations.
  • Security and compliance. Data protected with comprehensive enterprise controls.
  • 회의 참여 향상 / 효율적인 이메일 관리 / 글쓰기 지원
  • 프레젠테이션 개발 / 데이터 분석 및 시각화(visualization)
  • 보안 및 규정 준수(compliance)

⚙️ Step-by-Step Architecture

Copilot uses your organization's data — calendar events, emails, chats, documents, meetings from Microsoft Graph. Data remains secure within the Microsoft 365 service boundary.

1
User Prompt. Copilot receives an input prompt from a user in an app, such as Word or PowerPoint.
2
Grounding. Copilot preprocesses the input to improve specificity. Only accesses data the individual user is authorized to access, based on Microsoft 365 role-based access controls.
3
LLM Processing. Copilot post-processes the LLM response — including responsible AI checks, security, compliance, and privacy reviews.
4
Response Returned. Copilot returns the response to the app, where the user can review and assess it.
  1. 사용자 프롬프트: 앱에서 입력 받음
  2. 그라운딩(Grounding): 프롬프트 구체성 향상. 사용자 권한 범위 내 데이터만 접근.
  3. LLM 처리 및 후처리: 책임감 있는 AI 검사·보안·컴플라이언스·개인정보 검토 포함
  4. 응답 반환: 앱으로 반환 후 사용자 검토

Core Components of Microsoft 365 Copilot

⏱4분🎯Beginner📌Microsoft Learn
💡 파란 밑줄 단어를 터치하면 뜻이 팝업됩니다!

Overview

Microsoft 365 Copilot isn't a single app; it's intelligent capabilities built into Microsoft 365 applications. Powered by Microsoft Graph, an orchestration service, LLMs, and Work IQ — all working together to deliver context-aware assistance.

Copilot은 단독 앱이 아닌, Microsoft 365 앱에 내장된 지능형 기능들의 집합입니다. Microsoft Graph, 오케스트레이션(orchestration) 서비스, LLM, Work IQ가 함께 작동합니다.

Large Language Models (LLMs)

LLMs specialize in understanding and generating human-like text. They are a type of generative AI — instead of merely predicting or classifying, they can produce entirely new content. Microsoft's Azure OpenAI Service privately hosts these models, ensuring privacy and data integrity.

LLM은 인간과 유사한 텍스트를 이해하고 생성하는 AI 모델입니다. 생성형(generative) AI의 일종으로 새로운 콘텐츠를 생성합니다. Microsoft의 Azure OpenAI Service가 비공개로 호스팅하여 데이터 무결성(integrity)을 보장합니다.

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Key NLP components in Copilot:

  • Tokenization. Breaks down text into smaller chunks like words or phrases.
  • Semantic Analysis. Helps Copilot understand the underlying meaning or context.
  • Sentiment Analysis. Assesses the mood or emotion behind a text.
  • Language Translation. Aids in multilingual tasks across different languages.
  • 토큰화(Tokenization): 텍스트를 단어·구 같은 작은 단위로 분해
  • 의미 분석(Semantic Analysis): 기본 의미와 맥락 파악
  • 감정 분석(Sentiment Analysis): 텍스트 뒤의 감정·기분 평가
  • 언어 번역(Language Translation): 다국어 지원

Microsoft Graph + Work IQ

Microsoft Graph is the backbone of Copilot's intelligence — a unified data layer connecting emails, files, meetings, chats across Microsoft 365 into a single, secure framework.

Work IQ adds an extra layer of intelligence. Rather than treating files, emails, chats, meetings, and tasks as isolated data, Work IQ learns patterns, relationships, workflows, and preferences across everything you do.

⚠️ Important
Prompts, responses, and data accessed through Microsoft Graph are NOT used to train foundation LLMs.

Microsoft Graph는 Copilot 지능의 근간 — 이메일·파일·회의·채팅을 하나의 안전한 프레임워크로 연결합니다.

Work IQ는 파일·이메일·채팅·회의·작업의 패턴, 관계, 워크플로, 선호도를 학습합니다.

⚠️ 중요: Microsoft Graph를 통해 접근한 데이터는 LLM 훈련에 사용되지 않습니다.

Copilot Chat

Copilot Chat is the shared chat experience enabling cross-app intelligence. You can:

  • Catch up on things. Synthesize and summarize large amounts of data into simple summaries.
  • Create content and brainstorm. Draft content from storyboards to executive summaries.
  • Get quick answers. Your personal search engine — ask about files, messages, or forgotten information.
  • 따라잡기: 대량 데이터를 간단한 요약으로 합성
  • 콘텐츠 생성 및 브레인스토밍: 스토리보드부터 임원 요약본까지 초안 작성
  • 빠른 답변: 파일·메시지·잊어버린 정보에 대해 질문

Responsible AI Practices

⏱3분🎯Beginner📌Microsoft Learn
💡 파란 밑줄 단어를 터치하면 뜻이 팝업됩니다!

Six Principles of Responsible AI

Microsoft integrates AI with a strong emphasis on ethics and responsibility. The guiding principles:

⚖️
Fairness
Eliminate biases, equal treatment for all users.
🛡️
Reliability & Safety
Rigorous testing for consistent, safe performance.
🔒
Privacy & Security
Data protected in training and post-deployment.
🌍
Inclusiveness
AI accessible and beneficial for everyone.
🔍
Transparency
Keep users informed about how AI works.
📋
Accountability
Ethical and legal standards at the forefront.
  • ⚖️ 공정성(Fairness): 편향 제거, 동등 대우
  • 🛡️ 신뢰성 및 안전(Reliability & Safety): 일관성 있고 안전한 성능
  • 🔒 개인정보 보호(Privacy & Security): 훈련·배포 후 데이터 보호
  • 🌍 포용성(Inclusiveness): 모두가 접근 가능한 AI
  • 🔍 투명성(Transparency): AI 작동 방식 공개
  • 📋 책임감(Accountability): 윤리적·법적 기준 최우선

Microsoft's Strategy

Microsoft engineers build AI adhering to the Responsible AI Standard v2. Security experts protect confidentiality, integrity, and availability of AI systems from malicious attacks. Microsoft harnesses:

  • Ethical AI principles
  • Compliance standards for responsible AI
  • Comprehensive AI research including privacy-centric machine learning

An interdisciplinary team continually refines AI systems — enhancing training data, filtering harmful content, and addressing biases.

Microsoft 엔지니어들은 책임감 있는 AI 표준 v2를 준수합니다. 보안 전문가들이 기밀성(confidentiality)·무결성(integrity)·가용성(availability)을 보호합니다.

학제간 팀이 훈련 데이터 강화, 유해 콘텐츠 필터링, 편향(biases) 해결에 나섭니다.
